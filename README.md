# just-colorful-screen
Simple browser utility used to find broken/stuck pixels on screens.

---

## Status
Development finished, not activetely maintained.

---

## Features
Filling the screen with a defined color to search for broken pixels.

---

## Requirements
- Web browser such as chrome or firefox.

---

## Installing and usage
Download "just-colorful-screen.html", and launch it with your browser.

Or visit <a href="https://quadakr.github.io/just-colorful-screen/just-colorful-screen.html" target="blank">online page</a>.

Press f11 (or fn+f11 on most laptops) on the page, and hover your cursor over the colored buttons.

Search for anomalies on the screen.
